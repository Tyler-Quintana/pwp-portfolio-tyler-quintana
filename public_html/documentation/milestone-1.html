Purpose, Audience and Goal

Purpose

My PWP will serve as a location where I can display my works and accomplishments to potential future employers, fellow developers and my peers. Having my PWP be an ever-growing portfolio of my skill set will be very valuable to my future prospects.

Audience

The people I envision using this site are potential employers, professional peers and possible customers. This can also include any business who would be interested in an internship opportunity. This site will be designed for individuals who are looking at my projects and wish to see some evidence of the work I have completed and what skills I already know and can bring to a team.

Goal

The goal of this PWP is to either find employment in the field post graduation, or help me get into an internship for a company. The goal is the move forward will my career in one way or another and this PWP can be a really useful asset to have when entering the next phase of starting a career.
