Content Strategy

My personal website will be one page, and will have the following sections:

Introduction

This will display my full name as well as my role as a front end fullstack web developer. The background will have a picture of something such as mountains.

Get To Know Me

This will be a brief description of who I am and what my experience is. This is where I can explain how I am going through the bootcamp as well as list out what technical and soft skills I possess. It will be important to list and display in a clear way what languages I know as well as other important skills (such as git and github). This will come across as professional, with maybe a slight hint of humor to make it relatable.

My Projects

This will display my work via images and descriptions of each project. It will contain a brief explanation as to what each project was intended for, how it was created as well as what software and languages where used.

Contact Me

This will be a section where individuals can fill out a contact form in order to get in touch with me if they are interested. I will also have links to my professional email address, my GitHub as well as my LinkedIn.
