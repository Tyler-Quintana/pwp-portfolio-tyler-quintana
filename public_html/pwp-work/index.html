Get To Know Me

My name is Tyler Quintana. I am an Albuquerque based Fullstack Website Developer. I started learning Web Development in 2021 by teaching myself the basics. I decided I wanted to dive head first into the industry and jumped into the CNM Deep Dive Bootcamp in 2022. I enjoy taking on challenges that I know will push me out of my comfort zone to grow as a person. That aspect of me is what made me believe I should give coding a try!

Going through the bootcamp has helped me increase my skills with languages such as HTML and CSS. As well as teach me entirely new skill sets such as using JavaScript, git, GitHub, Node, the command line, Bootstrap, and MySQL. It has also helped me with my soft skills such as what it is really like to work with a team on big projects. I look forward to continuing my coding journey in the next stage of my career! If you think I would be a good fit for your team, or would like to get to know me, send me a message.
